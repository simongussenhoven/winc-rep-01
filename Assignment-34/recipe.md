#Roasted Veg Pasta Bake
##Ingredients
 - 500 g wholemeal penne
 - 2 cans of 400g chopped tomatoes
 - 2 red peppers
 - 1 yellow pepper
 - 1 medium leek
 - 2 red onions
 - 3 cloves of garlic
 - 1 courgette
 - 500 ml oat milk
 - 3 tbsp white flour
 - oil
 - salt & pepper
 - 1 tbsp maple syrup
 - 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
